About Me

Business Analyst | Product Owner | MSc Business Analytics Candidate

Who I Am

I’m a Business Analyst with hands-on Product Management experience, currently pursuing my MSc in Business Analytics at Dublin City University. I relocated to Ireland in September 2025 to begin my postgraduate journey and deepen my expertise in analytics, machine learning, and strategic decision-making.

My professional identity sits at the intersection of business analysis, product ownership, and data-driven strategy. I’ve spent the early years of my career working closely with engineering teams, stakeholders, and end users — understanding problems deeply before shaping the solutions that address them.

Professional Background

Before starting my MSc, I worked extensively as a Business Analyst and Scrum Product Owner in fast-paced Agile environments. My work typically involved:

NoteCore Responsibilities
  • Gathering and analysing business requirements from cross-functional stakeholders
  • Owning and prioritising product backlogs in Scrum teams
  • Translating business needs into user stories, acceptance criteria, and sprint goals
  • Collaborating daily with developers, designers, and QA to deliver iterative value
  • Using data to validate product decisions and measure outcomes

This foundation gave me a practical understanding of how products are actually built — the trade-offs, the stakeholder dynamics, and the importance of clear communication. It also showed me where analytics and ML can elevate product decisions from intuition-based to evidence-based.

Why an MSc in Business Analytics

After several years in business analysis and product ownership, I became increasingly aware of a gap in my toolkit: while I could facilitate decisions and translate between business and technical teams, I wanted to personally own the analytical and machine learning work that drives modern product strategy.

The MSc Business Analytics at DCU is where I’m building that capability — covering machine learning, statistical modelling, business transformation, new venture strategy, and AI ethics. I’m combining my prior experience with new technical depth to position myself for roles that go beyond traditional BA work.
